About Me

introduction

Greetings! My name is Philip, and I'm a Dutch 🇳🇱 second-year college student game development program at the HKU (Utrecht University of the Arts). I'm incredibly passionate about creating music, making art, mastering video editing, and exploring new skills and knowledge.

Interests

I draw inspiration from the stylish aesthetics of the 30s, envisioning big band scenes where everyone wears hats. I'm particularly fascinated by games like Cuphead and
anime like Spy x Family for their captivating art styles reminiscent of the 30-50s era.
Outside my game development studies is music which I make in Fl Studio, my other big passion. I spend a lot of time composing and experimenting with different sounds. When it comes to art and design, I strive to create pieces that evoke joy, using vibrant colors and incorporating subtle humor. In terms of coding, I'm always seeking to enhance the modularity and efficiency of my code, often exploring design patterns to optimize my projects.

Journey

My journey into the world of coding began after I learned 3D modeling in Blender. It sparked the idea of integrating my models into games, leading me to discover game jams. Through these events, I faced various challenges in game development, but my passion for music, coding, and art ultimately guided me towards pursuing game development as my college major.
