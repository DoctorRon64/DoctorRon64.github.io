My Journey

My dad once introduced me to the coding world only after I had learned how to 3D model in Blender. I thought,

"Wouldn't it be cool if I could use these models in a game?"
That's how I discovered the world of game jams. I participated in one, and then another, and after the summer break, I had to decide what I should pursue in college. As my passion for music-making, coding, and art-related things grew, and I faced many struggles with coding games, I realized that my opportunity lay in game development. This would truly combine my passions, and that is the reason I started Game Development at the HKU in september 2022.

My Interests
