About Me

Hello! My name is DrRon. I am a Dutch 🇳🇱 game developer and am very passionate, creative, and love learning new things. I am currently in my second year of my game development program at HKU in Utrecht. I'm very inspired by games like Cuphead and anime like Spy x Family because of their 20th-century style. In my spare time, I enjoy composing music with FL Studio and am trying to improve myself.

As a coder, I'm always trying to improve readability and efficiency in my code. That's why I recently developed a strong interest in design patterns and aim to learn and improve more in that area. I have specialized in AI as I have created numerous state machines and behavior trees during my time at HKU.

My dad introduced me to the coding world only after I had learned how to 3D model in Blender. I thought, "Wouldn't it be cool if I could use these models in a game?" That's how I discovered the world of game jams. I participated in one, and then another, and after the summer break, I had to decide what I should pursue in college. As my passion for music-making, coding, and art-related things grew, and I faced many struggles with coding games, I realized that my opportunity lay in game development.
